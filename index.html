    About Pepecoin (Pepe Coin)

    Pepecoin (also known as Pepe Coin) is the world's first fully decentralized and secure blockchain for Pepe. No one owns or controls Pepecoin. Consensus is achieved through a simple set of rules followed by all nodes on the network. As a true pepe meme coin, it is designed to be community-focused and censorship-resistant, offering a fast, low-fee, and reliable digital currency for everyone.

    Triple Minable: Litecoin, Dogecoin, and Pepecoin can be mined simultaneously using merged mining.

    Quick Facts

    • Pepecoin is a fully decentralized blockchain and true pepe meme coin.
    • It uses Proof-of-Work (PoW) forever—no staking, no smart contracts.
    • Pepecoin is merge-mined with Litecoin and Dogecoin for extra security.
    • Transaction fees are extremely low.
    • All code is open source.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is a Ribbit?

    A Ribbit is the smallest unit of Pepe Coin.

    0.00000001 Pepecoin = 1 Ribbit

    What does a Pepecoin address look like?

    Pepecoin addresses start with P. Here are a few examples:

    PoAGLLWhs3B6C6nJL26wEG79DuK6jHYP28
    Phm5pRuHTtJC7sULj2p6J48Bs8jY5xARHF
    PtSorBvbsuL7TNshbMQ1bc2PnRneLgezsg

    DO NOT send Pepecoin to these addresses.

    What is the currency symbol for Pepecoin?

    The Ᵽ character ("P with Stroke") is the official currency symbol for Pepecoin.

    Who created Pepecoin?

    Pepecoin was created by two developers, Dogecoin Shibes, and brothers, Zordiak and Karzo.

    Since launch, many members of the community have stepped up to join the team and help make Pepecoin a reality. Pepecoin is truly a community cryptocurrency.

    Coins vs Tokens

    There are two basic types of cryptocurrencies: coins and tokens. Tokens exist as smart contracts on a parent network (like Ethereum). Coins have their own blockchain.

    Pepecoin is a coin with its own blockchain. It uses Proof-of-Work just like Bitcoin. You can think of Pepecoin as "Bitcoin with a frog logo".

    Why was Pepecoin created?

    Most Pepe cryptocurrencies are tokens. Pepecoin was created with a bigger vision—a true Pepe Coin with its own blockchain and extremely low fees, perfect for sending money globally or making donations.

    When did Pepecoin launch?

    Pepecoin was fairly launched on January 30th, 2024.

    Do I have to trust the devs?

    No! Pepecoin is trustless. No one owns or controls Pepecoin. It is completely decentralized, just like Bitcoin.

    Is Pepecoin Core safe to use?

    Yes! All Pepecoin Core releases are cryptographically proven to be built from source and verified by multiple developers to ensure tamper-proof binaries.

    Why are there so many Pepe cryptocurrencies?

    It is easier than ever to create a token. Anyone can make a logo and launch a token these days, but Pepecoin is a true coin with its own blockchain.

    When will Pepecoin be listed on Uniswap, Metamask, etc?

    Like Bitcoin and Dogecoin, Pepecoin is not compatible with Web3 services.

    What is the contract address for Pepecoin?

    There is no contract address for Pepecoin. It operates on its own blockchain.

    Was Pepecoin created as a joke?

    No. Pepecoin was created with the intent to be the best option for a Pepe cryptocurrency. It is a fair-launch chain with low fees and triple mining support.

    I have another Pepe cryptocurrency, is that related to this one?

    No. Pepecoin is not affiliated with any other Pepe cryptocurrency. It was fairly launched on January 30th, 2024.

    How do I know if the Pepe cryptocurrency I have is this one?

    Check the address. It should start with the letter P. If your address starts with 0x, it's not Pepecoin. Pepecoin doesn't use smart contracts. If it has a contract address, it's not Pepecoin.

    What do you mean by "community-focused"?

    The Doge meme has a single coin and community. Pepecoin aims to unite the Pepe meme community around one chain and coin. r/Pepecoin

    What cryptocurrency was Pepecoin created from?

    Pepecoin is a code fork of Dogecoin for reliability, merged mining, and existing ecosystem.

    Is Pepecoin a hard fork of Dogecoin?

    No. Pepecoin is not a hard fork. It started as a brand new blockchain at block height 0.

    Is Pepecoin a revived chain?

    No, Pepecoin is not a revived chain. It is a brand new chain launched at block height 0.
